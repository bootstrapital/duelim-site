Close the Authority Gap

Our approach is rooted in Answer Engine Optimization (AEO), the strategic discipline for closing the Authority Gap. AEO is not traditional marketing; it is the practice of making your firm's expertise legible, verifiable, and authoritative to the AI systems and search engines that now shape your client's journey.

Building true authority is a long-term strategic commitment, not a short-term campaign. Our engagement model is designed to reflect this reality. We separate the initial, heavy-lifting phase from the ongoing management to align our work—and your investment—directly with the value being delivered at each stage.

Phase 1: The AEO Foundation & Roadmap

Timeline: 60-90 Days | Structure: Fixed-Fee Project

We begin with a one-time, fixed-fee project with a clear beginning and end. This allows us to deliver significant, tangible progress and build a foundation of trust before moving to a long-term partnership.

Scope & Deliverables:

  • A comprehensive "Authority Gap" audit to diagnose exactly where and why your firm is losing ground to AI and competitors.
  • Full implementation of Pillar I: Technical Authority, creating the machine-readable foundation essential for AEO success.
  • A strategic content roadmap for Pillar II: Content Authority, developed after a deep dive into your firm's existing intellectual capital.
  • A clear action plan for Pillar III: Entity Authority, identifying which key partners and topics to prioritize for building verifiable digital reputation.

The final deliverable is more than a document; it's a fully implemented technical foundation and a strategic roadmap for the next 6-12 months of execution.

Phase 2: The Authority Retainer

Timeline: Ongoing | Structure: Monthly Retainer (6-Month Minimum)

Following the foundational project, we transition to a monthly retainer for the ongoing execution of the strategic roadmap. This ensures the consistent, sustained effort required to build market-leading authority.

Scope & Deliverables:

  • Content Execution: Ongoing creation and publication of authoritative articles, guides, and Q&As based on the strategic roadmap.
  • Entity Management: Proactive management and enhancement of the digital profiles for the firm and its key experts to build their verifiable authority.
  • Performance Analytics: Monthly reporting on key business outcomes, tracking growth in organic visibility, SERP feature ownership, and the quality of inbound inquiries.
  • Quarterly Strategy Review: A dedicated session every three months to analyze performance, adapt to market changes, and refine the strategic roadmap.
"Building true authority doesn't happen overnight. Our process is designed for that reality. We start with a 90-day Foundational Project to do the heavy lifting... From there, we move to a monthly Authority Retainer to execute that plan. We require a minimum 6-month commitment because our goal is to deliver a real business outcome—making you the source of truth in your domain—and that requires sustained, consistent effort."

While we structure our retainers monthly, we are happy to offer a discount for clients who prefer to pay quarterly or semi-annually.
